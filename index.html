Research Experience

Fenster Lab: Image guidance in oncology

Two-time NSERC USRA Fellow

2021 - Present

Lab website

I am developing the world's first integrated prostate biopsy system using ultrasound and prostate-specific PET guidance. The system has the potential to provide a new method for identifying and localizing tumours using a novel compact imaging platforms and newly developed PET tracers. It will allow for improved detection and biopsy of early and recurring prostate cancers.

View project

Previously, I independently designed and tested the mechanical supporting device for a novel 3D-ultrasound guidance mechanism. The device will streamline procedures using an existing prostate biopsy system turning a 5-step process into 1 step.

Western Formula Racing

WFR represents Western University and Canada on the world stage. Every year, our team of more than 50 students designs and manufactures an single-seater electric race car, taking it to international competitions where we compete against teams from other universities. The competitions test the team's design and ability to communicate strategic and technical decisions to a panel of judges, and test the vehicle's performance with a series of on-track races. Western regularly gets invited to events across the globe and is recognised for being one of the best teams in North America.
